This project aims to improve public transportation access and interaction in the Washington Metropolitan Area by leveraging technology. We strive to provide a user-friendly interface for trip planning and updates on transportation news and disruptions. Our real-time information helps users make informed travel decisions. Our project aims to enhance the commuting experience for residents and visitors, making it a go-to platform for public transportation needs.
